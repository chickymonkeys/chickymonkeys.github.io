Welcome to my website! I am an applied economist and currently a postdoctoral scholar at the [Department of Economics](https://www.economics.ku.dk/) of the [University of Copenhagen](https://www.ku.dk/english/).

I received my PhD in Economics from the [Norwegian School of Economics (NHH)](https://www.nhh.no/en/departments/economics/).

My research focuses on the use of micro-econometric tools and data science techniques to investigate on topics in political economy and its intersection with relevant disciplines such as religion, economic history, and finance.
